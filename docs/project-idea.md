# Project Idea

## Name
VerifyNews

## Main idea
VerifyNews is a browser extension that helps users evaluate the reliability of political information while browsing online.

## Problem
Young users are exposed to political content online but often do not have the time, motivation, or tools to verify whether this information is reliable.

## Solution
The extension displays a reliability score directly on political webpages and allows users to access more detailed explanations, signals of doubt, and trusted sources.

## Target audience
Young users (18–25) who use digital tools naturally but feel distant from politics because it seems too complex or overwhelming.

## Main value
Fast, visual, simple and transparent support for political information verification.
